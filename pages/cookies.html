Cookie policy

What are cookies?

"Cookies (also known as internet cookies, web cookies, browser cookies or HTTP cookies) are small pieces of information in the form of a text file, containing a string of letters and numbers. This file is sent from a website to the person’s browser where they get saved before going back to the same website."

Source: www.internetcookies.com.

For simplicity, "cookie" here refers to cookies as such but also to any alternative allowing information to be stored at browser level and to be returned during browsing or at a later date. The use of local storage or "pixels" are examples of alternative techniques.

What are cookies used for on this website?

  • Session management: Some part of the website requires you to be logged in.
  • Personalization: Some part of the website uses cookies to store user data.

What cookies are not used for on this website?

Basically everything else that is not listed above (ie. tracking, third-party cookies, ...)
